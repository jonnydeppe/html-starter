About YGW

YGW (You Got Work) is the ultimate tasking platform for the decentralized era. Inspired by the underground networks of the dark web and powered by cutting-edge AI, YGW offers a secure, private, and efficient way for individuals to hire and work anonymously.

At its core lies "Table," a rogue AI that ensures fairness, anonymity, and precise task matching. Whether you're looking to hire skilled workers or offer your unique talents, YGW provides the infrastructure to thrive in a world free from surveillance and control.
