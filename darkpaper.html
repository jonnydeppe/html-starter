The modern world marginalizes those who do not conform. It undervalues skills that fall outside predefined roles and leaves capable individuals behind. Platforms designed to connect people instead confine them, leaving little room for true freedom.

  1. Centralized Systems Fail the Unique: They impose their rules, their fees, and their vision, erasing those who refuse to fit their mold.
  2. Privacy is a Myth: Surveillance and exploitation are the hallmarks of modern platforms.
  3. No Room for the Exceptional: Those with niche, high-risk, or unconventional skills find no place in mainstream systems.

The result? Millions of valuable people left adrift, with no platform to realize their true potential.

4. Core Features

4.1 Dark Web Exclusivity:

  • YGW operates entirely as a .onion site, accessible via Tor Browser, ensuring absolute privacy and security.

4.2 Autonomous Validation with Table:

  • Table assesses every task, user, and transaction with impartial precision.
  • It ensures tasks are appropriately priced, evaluates skill credibility, and rejects anything that does not meet its rigorous standards.

4.3 Task Management:

  • Hire Flow: Table guides hirers through task creation, ensuring fair compensation and risk alignment.
  • Work Flow: Table matches tasks to workers with precision, prioritizing verified skills and ranks.
  • Recognition Systems: Commendations and tipping reward exceptional contributions, enhancing reputation.

4.4 Communication and Data Security:

  • Encrypted Communications: All communications are encrypted, ensuring privacy and security.
  • Temporary Messaging: Messages, GPS data, and task-related information are automatically destroyed upon task completion.
  • AI Moderation: Table monitors chats to strip away personal identifiers (e.g., phone numbers, emails). After task completion, involved accounts are automatically banned to maintain privacy and security.

4.5 Ranking and Influence:

  • Ranks are determined by token holdings, task performance, and commendations. Your rank defines your influence in the system.
