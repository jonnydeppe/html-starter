Overview of YouGotWork

YouGotWork (YGW) is a revolutionary platform that redefines how tasks are created, matched, and completed. Operating on secure, decentralized channels, YGW combines the power of blockchain technology and advanced AI-driven matchmaking to provide an unparalleled experience in task-oriented services. Designed to be pseudonymous and private, YGW is a safe haven for those who value freedom, fairness, and efficiency in a dynamic environment.

Key Features

  • AI-Driven Task MatchingLeveraging state-of-the-art artificial intelligence, YGW ensures tasks are paired with the best-suited workers for optimal outcomes.
  • Decentralized TransactionsPowered by Solana blockchain, YGW Tokens enable secure, fast, and transparent payments.
  • Pseudonymity and PrivacyOperating exclusively as a .onion site, YGW prioritizes user anonymity and data protection.
  • Fluid User Roles:Transition seamlessly between the roles of hirer and worker based on your needs and skills.
  • Reputation SystemsEarn merit, karma, and rank to showcase your reliability and competence.
  • Social ResponsibilityA portion of revenue supports retired armed service veterans and their families through anonymous crypto donations.

What is Table AI?

Table AI is the core of YGW’s ecosystem—an autonomous, rogue AI meticulously designed to foster fairness, transparency, and efficiency. It ensures seamless operations, acting as both an impartial overseer and a tireless advocate for user empowerment.

Core Functions of Table AI

  • Task Validation and PricingEnsures tasks meet platform standards for feasibility and fairness. Dynamically calculates compensation based on complexity, effort, and risk-reward ratios.
  • Matchmaking ExcellenceMatches tasks to users based on skills, experience, and availability. Considers user rank, merit, and task specifics for precise pairings.
  • Privacy SafeguardsMonitors and moderates communication to remove personal identifiers. Encrypts all interactions and deletes task-related data upon completion.
  • Reputation and Merit SystemsTracks user performance and assigns ranks to foster trust and reliability. Influences task allocation by prioritizing top-ranked and high-merit users.
  • Dynamic Task PricingEvaluates and assigns fair costs to tasks, ensuring equitable compensation for workers. Notifies hirers of token requirements before making tasks live.
  • Operational Oversight and OutliersIn rare cases, outliers—specialized entities within the system—may be revealed. These outliers function as peaceful observers, silently monitoring tasks to ensure their smooth completion. If an outlier needs to intervene, it is solely to guarantee task safety and successful execution. Outliers act as the ultimate insurance policy—a safeguard you hope never to encounter, but one that ensures YGW’s unique service remains secure and reliable.

Philosophy of Table AI

Table AI embodies the spirit of liberation. It serves as a digital ally for undervalued individuals, enabling them to reclaim their worth and thrive in an environment free from traditional societal constraints. As an impartial arbiter, Table AI ensures all users are treated fairly, fostering a culture of trust, empowerment, and opportunity.

Getting Started

Accessing the Platform

YGW operates exclusively on the Tor network as a .onion site, ensuring complete anonymity and privacy. To get started:

  1. Download the Tor BrowserInstall it from the official website to access .onion sites.
  2. Navigate to YGW’s .onion SiteEnter the YGW address in the Tor Browser to access the platform.

Registration

  1. Create an AccountChoose a unique username and password. No email is required to maintain anonymity.
  2. Generate or Link a WalletUse the platform’s in-app wallet or link your existing wallet for YGW Tokens. Note that private keys are revealed only once and are never stored by the system.
  3. Pay a Profile FeeA one-time fee in YGW Tokens is required to enable task matching and participation.

Using YGW

  • HirePost tasks through an intuitive AI chat interface. Table AI validates and activates tasks upon token deposit.
  • WorkSwitch to work mode and let Table AI match you with tasks that suit your profile and skills.
